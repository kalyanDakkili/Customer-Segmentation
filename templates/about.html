About This Project

Customer Segmentation Analysis

This application uses K-Means clustering to group customers based on their demographic and behavioral characteristics. The clustering helps businesses understand different customer segments and tailor their marketing strategies accordingly.

How It Works

The system analyzes three key factors:

  • Age: The customer's age in years
  • Annual Income: The customer's yearly income in thousands of dollars
  • Spending Score: A score from 1-100 representing the customer's spending behavior
